Hairloss

23 November, 2013

There are many causes of hair loss; the homeopathic clinic is well aware of this fact and we go to great lengths to determine the underlying problem and then provide specific treatments.

Androgenetic alopecia (pattern baldness) is the most common cause of hair loss in both men and women. It is the medical description for pattern hair loss influenced by genetic factors. Men generally suffer a much greater degree of loss due to this condition. They classically develop a receding hair line and grow a bald spot in the middle of the scalp. In women the pattern of hair loss is typically more diffuse. Pattern baldness is strongly influenced by the interaction of our genes with particular hormones. Recent medical research has attributed a large degree of blame for this hair-loss to hormone dihydrotestosterone. Hair follicles vulnerable to this hormone start to produce smaller, thinner and less pigmented hair. There are many other causes of hair loss, some of these include alopecia areata, telogen effluvium and anagen effluvium hormonal irregularities, skin infections and vitamin and nutritional deficiencies.

We understand the people with thinning hair have special requirements when comes to hair care products. To achieve better cosmetic appearance, thinning hair needs gentle care and extra volume.
